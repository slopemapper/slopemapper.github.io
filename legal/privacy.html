Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 January 2025

At Slopemapper ("we", "our", "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Slopemapper app and website (collectively, "Services"). We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and relevant UK data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

The Slopemapper app and website do not collect any personally identifiable information (“PII”). However, we may collect the following types of non-personal data to improve your experience:

  • Resort selection and route preferences
  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system)
  • App performance data

In the future, if we plan to collect PII (such as names, emails, or location data), we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Services
  • Analyse app performance and user behavior
  • Optimise app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure the security of the app and website

We do not sell, share, or disclose any data to third parties except as required to operate the Services (such as for legal compliance or technical support).

3. Legal Basis for Processing Data

Under GDPR, we process your non-personal data based on our legitimate interest in providing and improving the Slopemapper app and website. We do not process any personal data at this time.

4. Data Retention

We retain non-personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Once no longer needed, the data is securely deleted.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As the app does not collect PII, the following rights are not applicable in the current version of Slopemapper:

  • Right to Access
  • Right to Rectification
  • Right to Erasure
  • Right to Restrict Processing
  • Right to Data Portability
  • Right to Object

If we begin processing personal data in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. While we strive to ensure data security, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Third-Party Links

Our app and website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed of how we protect your data.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: slopemapperapp@gmail.com.
